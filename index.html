Let me introduce myself :)

As you already know, my name is Oleg Mordach, I'm QA engineer and I'm really excited about what I'm working on.

Fields of my profesional interests are: Quality Assuarance, Automation Testing and Continuous Integration.

I live and work at Lviv - beautifull city in western part of Ukraine. I like to go on hiking trips during weekends. It's not only exercises, but it allows me to enjoy of nature and get away from the cares of the day.

And what about my role at the company?

I'm responsible for the whole QA process in the team.

My typical work day begins with checking results of Automation testing. I'm using Behat with Mink to automate regression testing on our biggest laravel project and Jenkins + Docker for continuous integration.

Next, I have a meeting with the team where we discuss plans for today. After that, I usually create new or update my autotests, check new features manually, prepare tests documentation.

During the day I collaborate with our developers, explain them new tasks if needed, check statuses and progress of tasks.

Also, I'm preparing reports for our customers and take part in meetings with them, where I show our progress and discuss new tasks. In cooperation with PM, we plan development process and distribute tasks among dev team.
